Welcome Visitor to the [ openspace ] wilderness. The [ os ] wilderness is a nature preserve for a small section of abandoned internet. The wilderness was created by collecting the posts of forgotten blogs that have not been updated in 6 or more years. These neglected posts make up the majority of explorable terrain within the enclosure. Visitors are invited to navigate, tag, and discover the contents within the sizable bounds of the collection. Additionally, the park supports a dynamic population of roving predator and forager profiles that survive by consuming other profiles and grazing on the contents of the posts. The [ openspace ] wilderness is itself a dynamic and wild space - it may be preserved and catalogued or overrun and destroyed. It is an explorable plot of old open internet in which you are free to roam.
